### Duties
The Office of Military Education, National Defence Studies Institute, is responsible for the administration, planning, direction, coordination, supervision, and implementation of military education and training programs in alignment with the Royal Thai Armed Forces Headquarters' career paths or other assigned military courses. This includes graduate studies, the dissemination of military science, military library services, as well as the collection, development, and distribution of institutional knowledge, under the command and responsibility of the Director of the Office of Military Education.

### Vision
As a premier center for foundational military education, the Office of Military Education develops high-potential personnel to meet the professional standards trusted by the Royal Thai Armed Forces.

### Mission
1. Provide education and training courses in accordance with the career paths and educational science branches of the Royal Thai Armed Forces Headquarters.
2. Develop and manage military library operations.
3. Coordinate with relevant universities in accordance with Memorandums of Understanding (MoU).
4. Disseminate and exchange knowledge in military science and security studies.
5. Encourage personnel and trainees to uphold morality, ethics, and the core values of the Royal Thai Armed Forces Headquarters.

### Core Values
- **S = Service Mind:** Dedicated to serving with a volunteer spirit and sacrifice for the Nation, Religion, and King.
- **M = Mastery:** Excelling with expertise in foundational military education.
- **A = Altruism:** Prioritizing the common good.
- **A = Accountability:** Committed to responsibility, transparency, and accountability in all missions.
- **R = Reliability:** Maintaining credibility, honesty, and trustworthiness both internally and externally.
- **T = Teamwork:** Promoting a work culture of knowing one's duties, unity, kindness, and forgiveness.
- **OME = Office of Military Education:** Synergizing knowledge and morality to develop the organization into the premier foundational military education training center of the Royal Thai Armed Forces Headquarters.
