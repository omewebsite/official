### Divisions
The Directorate of Military Education comprises the Division of Administration, the Division of Training, and the Division of Technical Affairs.

### Division of Administration
Responsible for conducting and coordinating, supervising, and overseeing administrative affairs, records management, intelligence, personnel, security, civil affairs, finance, accounting, and budgeting within its purview, ensuring adherence to official regulations. This also includes preparing and procuring training aids and audiovisual equipment, maintaining school buildings and premises, and managing logistics.

### Division of Training
Responsible for managing training according to curricula in line with the Royal Thai Armed Forces Headquarters' (RTARF HQ) service guidelines for commissioned officers and non-commissioned officers. It also handles personnel matters related to the governance and command of students undergoing education. The division considers, recommends, and updates educational and training curricula to ensure they remain current. It conducts administrative tasks for education and training, and provides data for policy formulation and the development of plans and projects concerning education administration, education, and training, including the creation of timetables.

### Division of Technical Affairs
Responsible for considering and proposing opinions, directing, coordinating, implementing, supervising, and overseeing a wide range of academic and educational functions. This includes the management of military science curricula and national security development curricula in both public and private universities. The division also handles educational information technology, library affairs, graduate school operations, and academic collaborations, including the preparation of domestic and international memoranda of understanding or agreements. Furthermore, its duties encompass the production of textbooks and media, statistical work, analysis, evaluation, research, curriculum quality assurance, military academic promotion, and the preparation of performance agreements for government service.
